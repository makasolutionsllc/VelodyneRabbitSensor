Velodyne Rabbit Control Package
===============================

-Includes required operational files for rabbit device.
-Includes required client files for control over rabbit and accessories.
-Note all device IPs are hard-coded, and found on bottom panel of rabbit system.

Note files are ordered by install location
==========================================

-Files with a prefix of RPI are loaded onto and executed within the raspberry pi located on the rabbit drive assembly.
-Files with a prefix of LOCAL are loaded onto and executed within the text PC used by the operator.
